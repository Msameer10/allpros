Sameerian Country Government's primary aim to make a more better and enriched society than yesterday and a more better place for next generations.We have taken a lot of steps and initializations to achieve our ideas and ambitions for people,society.

The country of Sameerian Country is a vast country with a population of 22,098,879 Bordered between frozen lands to the North, a tropical forest to the South, mineral rich mountains to the East and a calm river to the West, the country of Sameerian Country mainly lives off marketing,trading,education,information technology and many more apportunities.

Sameerian Country itself is mainly covered in plains and has a windy climate, which has lead to a crowded population, despite the number of people, which means most of them live in large villages and modern towns.

The country's landscape is mind boggling; bleak, bare forests, ominous trees and ice-cold mountains are just a sliver of the barrenness Sameerian Country has to offer, which is why the country is scorned among foreigners. The people of Sameerian Country are toughtful towards foreigners and tend to welcome them with a place to stay. They feel foreigners could maintain the country's well being.

Sameerian Country has realistic laws and law enforcement, which makes it a dream country to live in for many. The people are bidded to be happiest in the world.

This is also reflected in the country's flag, which has two vertical stripes and a star in brown, light yellow and dark yellow. The coat of arms is a Rising Phoneix.
